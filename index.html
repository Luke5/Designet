About Us

Designet is a business and employment-oriented social networking service especially for designers of all kinds. It allows easy communication and interaction by and between companies and professional designers. Sign up and you will be able to better connect to professionals and business, allowing you to better communicate digitally and work in real-time on all aspects of design.
Companies and professionals will have full flexibility of their visual profiles and portfolios on the website. The ability to bid in real-time on freelance and contract work provides huge potential for quicker creative content creation and launches, too.
